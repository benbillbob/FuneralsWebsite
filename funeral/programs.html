Church Funerals

  • Prelude: Gentle welcome, usually 20 mins instrumental (organ or violin).
  • Entrance Song: Song or hymn for procession (e.g. Ave Maria, Amazing Grace).
  • Offertory Hymn: Psalm after a reading (e.g. The Lord is My Shepherd).
  • Communion Hymn: Hymns for congregation (e.g. On Eagles Wings, Abide with Me).
  • Song During Slideshow: Special song (e.g. How Great Thou Art, Danny Boy).
  • Recessional: Uplifting song for departure (e.g. Time to Say Goodbye, You’ll Never Walk Alone).

Cremation Services

  • Prelude: Calming welcome (instrumental or soft song).
  • Procession: Music for entrance (e.g. Morning Has Broken, Fields of Gold).
  • During: Favourite song during service.
  • Hymns: Optional religious music.
  • Committal: Reflective piece (e.g. The Rose, The Last Rose of Summer).
  • Recessional: Joyous song to conclude (e.g. You Raise Me Up, Wind Beneath My Wings).

Outdoor Funerals & Burials

We have a small battery powered sound system that we can provide for outdoor services. It amplifies the music so that it is loud enough to be heard clearly but not too loud for a funeral service.
